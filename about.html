About CAMPUS ONE

A government-inspired CBSE school committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

Our History

Established in 2008 with a vision to deliver inclusive and high-quality education, CAMPUS ONE began its journey as a small institution with just 120 students. Over the years, it has evolved into a dynamic and thriving academic community, now nurturing over 1450 learners. Built on a strong foundation of academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development, CAMPUS ONE is committed to shaping well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of a rapidly changing world. The institution emphasizes a balanced approach to education, integrating rigorous academics with co-curricular and life-skill development. Through continuous innovation, dedicated faculty, and a student-centered environment, CAMPUS ONE has established itself as a trusted name in modern education, fostering confidence, creativity, and leadership among its students.

Key Milestones:
• 2008 - Founded
• 2012 - CBSE Affiliation granted
• 2020 - Smart Campus initiative launched
• 2025 - 100% board results for 8 consecutive years
